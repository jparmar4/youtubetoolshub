## Content Guidelines

### SEO Best Practices
1. **Title Format:** Include year (2026), power words, numbers when applicable
2. **Meta Descriptions:** 155-160 characters, include primary keyword
3. **Headers:** H2s every 300-500 words, H3s for subsections
4. **Internal Links:** 3-5 links to relevant tools per post
5. **External Links:** 2-3 authoritative sources (YouTube Help, IRS, etc.)

### High-CPC Optimization
1. Focus on money/finance-related topics (tax, income, business)
2. Target US audience explicitly in content
3. Use professional/business terminology
4. Include calculator tools and actionable data
5. Add FAQ sections with schema markup

### Publishing Schedule
- **Time:** 9:00 AM EST (peak US morning traffic)
- **Frequency:** Daily for 30 days
- **Promotion:** Share on social media, submit to IndexNow

### KPIs to Track
- Page RPM per article (target: $5-15)
- US traffic percentage (target: 60%+)
- Average session duration (target: 3+ minutes)
- Pages per session (target: 2+)
